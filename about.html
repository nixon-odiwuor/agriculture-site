Why DWB Foundation

Do it With Boldness Foundation is a duly registered non-profit organization committed to transforming lives and building resilient communities through education, mentorship, advocacy, leadership development, and economic empowerment initiatives. Founded in Kenya, the foundation focuses on empowering youths, young girls, and women by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, opportunities, and confidence needed to realize their full potential and become agents of positive change in society. Driven by the belief that sustainable development begins with empowered people, DWB works closely with communities to promote psychosocial support, gender equality, reproductive health awareness, entrepreneurship, mental wellness, and leadership growth. Through mentorship programs, community outreach, training workshops, partnerships, and volunteerism, the organization creates safe spaces where vulnerable and marginalized individuals can thrive socially, economically, and emotionally. With the guiding motto, “We Are Because You Are,” DWB continues to inspire hope, nurture resilience, and build a generation of empowered, enlightened, productive, and goal-oriented young people capable of shaping a better future for their communities and beyond
